LEGACY SQUASH INSTITUTE UGANDA

“Igniting Greatness, Defining Legacy”

Founded by: Ivan Wasswa & Paul Kasirye Kadoma

VISION & MISSION

Vision: To position Uganda as the hub of squash excellence and innovation in Africa.

Mission: To nurture, educate, and empower athletes through world-class training, values, and global exposure.

CONSTITUTION SUMMARY

Legacy Squash Institute Uganda (LSIU) is a registered non-profit sports academy under the National Council of Sports Act 2023 and affiliated with the Uganda Squash Rackets Association (USRA). It aims to develop elite squash athletes through structured training, community development, and international exposure. The Institute operates under a governance board, upholds integrity and safeguarding standards, and maintains full transparency in financial operations.
